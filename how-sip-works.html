How SIP Works: A Beginner's Guide

Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) is a method of investing in mutual funds where an investor chooses a mutual fund scheme and invests a fixed amount of money at fixed intervals.

The Power of Rupee Cost Averaging

When you invest via SIP, you buy more units when the market is low and fewer units when the market is high. Over time, this averages out the cost of your investment.

Key Benefits:

  • Disciplined investing
  • Convenience
  • Power of compounding
  • No need to time the market
